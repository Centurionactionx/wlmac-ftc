Who We Are

WLMAC Robotics is the student-run FIRST Tech Challenge program at William Lyon Mackenzie CI in North York, Ontario.

Two seasons in, we field two competitive teams: CyberLyons (#27964) and MechLyons (#32514). We build robots from scratch, run STEM outreach across the region, and compete at qualifiers across Ontario. FTC compresses an entire engineering org into a school year: design reviews, fabrication, code sprints, strategy. Students leave fluent in the workflow real engineering teams run on.

CyberLyons · #27964

Our programming-focused team handles controls, software architecture, and autonomous strategy. CyberLyons drives the technical intelligence behind every match, from sensor integration to competition-day decision making.

MechLyons · #32514

Our mechanical team specialises in robot design, fabrication, and physical systems. MechLyons takes ideas from whiteboard to field, prototyping, iterating, and building hardware that holds up under competition pressure.

Outreach

We've reached 200+ students across 5 outreach events and 3 partner schools. From hands-on robotics workshops at MSEO 2026 to our Library Open House and Grade 9 Night, we're actively building the local STEM pipeline.

Compete

We attend Ontario FTC qualifiers and push our robots and ourselves against the best teams in the region. Every competition is a full-team effort: drivers, programmers, strategists, and builders all on the floor together.

Partnership Tiers

We treat sponsorship like a partnership, not a donation.

Your brand moves with the robot, the team, the school, and every event we touch, from a 1,400-student campus to community demos across North York. Every partner gets a direct line to the team and a real seat at our season. Tell us what you can contribute and we'll match it to the right tier of recognition.

Tier I · Cub - $100-$499

Name on team website and sponsor wall. Recognition in our season recap post. A personalised thank-you from the team. A great entry point for local businesses who want to show up for their community.

Tier II · Challenger - $500-$1,499

Everything in Cub, plus logo on pit display and printed materials, a dedicated social media post, and an end-of-season impact report showing exactly where your support went.

Tier III · Innovator - $1,500-$2,499

Everything in Challenger, plus logo on team apparel and jerseys, prominent logo placement on a competing FTC robot, named in all season media, and an invite to our sponsor showcase event.

Tier IV · Lyon Champion - $2,500+

Our flagship partnership. Title sponsor on robot and jerseys, a live robot demonstration at your office or storefront, and a fully custom partnership package tailored to your brand goals. Maximum visibility, all season long.

Logo on Robot

Premium placement on a competing FTC robot, photographed and filmed throughout the entire season. Your logo travels to every qualifier, demo, and outreach event we attend across Ontario.

In-Kind & Parts Support

Can't do cash? Donated tools, parts, or services directly reduce our required funding and receive the same recognition as monetary contributions. Sponsor a robot bumper, supply electronics, or mentor a student. It all counts.
